### 📝 Notes
- **15a** focuses on sending data (sensor monitoring, debugging).
- **15b** teaches how to read strings/characters and parse simple commands.
- **15c** is a direct application of 15a & 15b for interactive LED control.
- All simulations can be opened by clicking **"Open"** (make sure the Tinkercad link is public).
